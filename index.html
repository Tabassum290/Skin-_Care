Skin Care Routine Guide

Morning Routine — Start Your Day Fresh

1.Cleanser

Use a gentle cleanser to remove oil and dirt that build up overnight.
Tip: Choose a hydrating cleanser if your skin is dry, or a foaming one if it’s oily.

2.Toner

Apply toner with a cotton pad or your hands to balance your skin’s pH and tighten pores.
Look for: Alcohol-free formulas with rose water or witch hazel.

3.Serum

Use a lightweight serum with Vitamin C or hyaluronic acid for hydration and glow.
Why: Serums deliver active ingredients deep into your skin.

4.Moisturizer

Lock in hydration with a moisturizer suitable for your skin type.
Tip: Gel-based for oily skin, cream-based for dry skin

5.Sunscreen(SPF 30+):

Never skip sunscreen — it protects your skin from UV damage and premature aging.Apply 15–20 minutes before going out.

Night Routine — Heal While You Sleep

1.Cleanser

Remove makeup, sunscreen, and impurities with a gentle cleanser.
Double cleanse if you wear makeup (oil-based cleanser first, then foam cleanser).

2.Exfoliate (2–3 times a week):

Gently scrub away dead skin cells to keep skin smooth and bright.
Avoid over-exfoliating; it can cause irritation.

3.Moisturizer / Night Cream:

Finish with a nourishing night cream or sleeping mask to seal in moisture.

Weekly Add-ons

Face Mask (1–2 times a week):For deep hydration or detox.

Facial Steaming:Opens pores and improves product absorption.

Spot Treatment:Use only on problem areas if needed.
